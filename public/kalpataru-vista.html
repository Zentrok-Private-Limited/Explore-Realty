Overview

Developer: Kalpataru Limited

Project Size: 2.7 acres

Location: Noida Sector 128, Noida-Greater Noida Expressway

Price: ₹5.42 Cr*

Contact Elite Team

Architecture

Contemporary luxury high-rise design with panoramic views of the golf course. Striking modern elevation with sleek glass façades. Grand double-height entrance lobbies that create a sense of arrival. Apartments designed with open layouts for natural light and ventilation. Large balconies / sundecks extending living spaces to the outdoors.

Theme & Interiors

Focus on luxury lifestyle living blended with modern functionality. Premium finishes including imported Italian marble flooring. International-style modular kitchens with premium fittings. VRV air-conditioning systems for climate comfort. Bathrooms with high-end sanitary fittings and finishes.

Landscape & Surroundings

Over ~70% open/green area with lush landscapes. Golf-course facing apartments ensuring scenic views. Dedicated zones for leisure, wellness, and community living.
