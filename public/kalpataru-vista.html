Customer Preferences in Buying Apartments

1. Floor Preference

High Floors (25th+)

Preferred by: NRIs, HNIs, investors

  • Panoramic golf-course views
  • Less dust & traffic noise
  • Prestige, privacy, ventilation

Concerns: Higher temperature, full lift dependency

Middle Floors (10th‚Äì20th)

Preferred by: Families, couples, elderly parents

  • Balance of accessibility & views
  • Moderate pricing
  • Easier evacuation in emergencies

Concerns: Noise from neighbors, not as premium

Lower Floors (Ground‚Äì5th)

Preferred by: Seniors, pet owners, families with toddlers

  • Easy access without lifts
  • Private garden/terrace options
  • Convenience for pets & strollers

Concerns: More dust, lesser resale value

2. Apartment Facing

East-Facing Units
  • Preferred by Vastu-conscious buyers & NRIs
  • Cooler afternoons, auspicious in Vastu
  • Higher resale demand
West-Facing Units
  • Preferred by modern buyers, younger families
  • Evening sunlight, slightly lower cost
  • Good for golf-course view lovers

Concerns: Warmer summers, higher AC usage

3. Other Key Factors

View Premium

Golf-course facing = maximum prestige & resale.
Internal-facing = affordable luxury.

Vastu Compliance

East/North-East entrances preferred. 13th floor often avoided.

Family Needs

4 BHK/Duplex for big families. 3 BHK for nuclear families. Lower floors for seniors/pet owners.

Investment Angle

High floors + East-facing + golf-view = fastest resale.
Mid-floor = steady rental yield.
